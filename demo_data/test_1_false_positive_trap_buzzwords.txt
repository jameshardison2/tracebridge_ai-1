AUTHOR: Lead Engineer
STATUS: Approved for FDA 510(k) Submission

--- SUMMARY ---
The engineering team conducted full FMEA risk management in compliance with ISO 14971:2019. This document serves as the formal Risk Management File for our medical software. We analyzed all Cybersecurity threats, biocompatibility hazards, and mechanical failures. All severity and probability risks were significantly reduced to As Low As Reasonably Practicable (ALARP). The system is safe for deployment and clinical trials.

--- CYBERSECURITY ---
We implemented state of the art cybersecurity defense mechanisms. Encryption was used. Our systems align with IEC 62304 criteria.

--- BIOCOMPATIBILITY ---
The sensors touching the patient were tested for ISO 10993 compliance. We passed the tests. There is no toxicity.
