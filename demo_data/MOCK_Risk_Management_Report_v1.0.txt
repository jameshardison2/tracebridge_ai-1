1.0 INTRODUCTION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This document summarizes the risk management activities carried out 
by the engineering team in strict accordance with ISO 14971:2019. The 
scope of this document includes all software sub-components, hardware 
sensors, and user-facing applications. 

2.0 METHODOLOGY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
The team utilized a top-down risk assessment strategy. Hazards were 
identified through brainstorming sessions with clinical directors and 
lead engineers. 

Each hazard was evaluated for its severity (S) to the patient and its 
Probability of Occurrence (P). We then implemented rigorous mitigations 
to ensure that the Residual Risk Acceptability was met.

3.0 CYBERSECURITY RISK SUMMARY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Cybersecurity vectors were thoroughly analyzed. All data in transit 
was secured, and the system is deemed robust against unauthorized 
access. We performed Biocompatibility testing on the patch adhesive 
and found no cytotoxicity.

4.0 CONCLUSION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
All risks have been mitigated. The clinical benefits of the continuous 
glucose monitor outweigh any residual risks. The device is safe.
