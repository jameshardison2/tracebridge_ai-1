SECTION V: Device Security
Our medical device is highly secure. We implemented AES-256 encryption on all Bluetooth channels to prevent hackers. The software engineering team promises that all third-party dependencies are perfectly safe and we don't use any bad open source code. 
We run an anti-virus scanner periodically.
